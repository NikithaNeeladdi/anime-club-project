UW Anime Club

About Us

UW Anime Club is a community of anime enthusiasts at the University of Washington who come together to celebrate Japanese animation and culture. Whether you're a seasoned otaku or just curious about anime, everyone is welcome to join us!

Our-Mission

Our mission is to create a welcoming and inclusive space for anime fans to connect, share their passion, and explore the diverse world of anime and manga. Through screenings, discussions, events, and social gatherings, we aim to foster a sense of camaraderie and appreciation for this art form.

What We Offer

  • Weekly Screenings
  • Discussion Sessions
  • Events and Workshops
  • Cosplay Contests
  • Community Building
